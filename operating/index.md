# Approach

In operating experiments, I focus on testing systems in the real world. I track how far I can reach on VHF, experiment with different antennas, and refine my technique using the Anytone AT-D878. Every session follows a simple loop: plan → execute → observe → reflect. I record QSOs, note signal quality, and compare setups so I can see what works and what doesn’t. The goal isn’t just making contacts—it’s to understand how equipment, environment, and technique interact, and to let each real-world observation guide my next steps in learning and experimentation.
