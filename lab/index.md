#lab

## Overall strategy

My lab work is about learning by doing. I build circuits, run tests, and carefully watch what happens, whether on a breadboard, with an Arduino, or using a Raspberry Pi. Each experiment follows a simple loop: question → setup → observe → insight. I stay focused and repeatable, but I also explore rabbit holes when they lead to useful understanding. The goal isn’t to complete projects, but to see how things behave, one small experiment at a time, letting each result guide what I try next.
