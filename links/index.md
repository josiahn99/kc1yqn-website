# Resources

A running list of resources that have actually moved me forward in learning radio, electronics, and how signals move through the world—used alongside my [RF Roadmap](/roadmap/). Not comprehensive, and not intended as a recommended path.

### 1. Inspiration

- *[Daniel and Kelly's Extraordinary Universe podcast](https://podcasts.apple.com/us/podcast/daniel-and-kellys-extraordinary-universe/id1436616330)*
- *[Remembrance of Earth's Past](https://www.amazon.com/Three-Body-Problem-Boxed-Set-Remembrance/dp/1250254493) by Cixin Liu*

### 2. Licensing and Getting Started

- [ARRL General Class License Manual](https://home.arrl.org/action/Store/Product-Details/productId/2009476073)
- [Amateur Repeater Directory](https://app.amateurrepeaterdirectory.org)
- [Repeaterbook.com](http://www.repeaterbook.com)
- [Radio Theory Handbook, Beginner - Advanced](https://www.amazon.com/Radio-Theory-Handbook-Beginner-Advanced/dp/B0BNV2PC6F) by Ron Bertrand, VK2DQ
- [QRZ.com](http://www.qrz.com)
