# Breadboard Experiments  
Hands-on circuits and bench experiments that connect electronics theory with real-world RF behavior. 

Based largely on going throught the tutorials on ["https://www.electronics-tutorials.ws/](Electronics-Tutorials.ws).

## Analog / Breadboard

** Astable Multivibrator ** 

- **RC Oscillator Frequency Analysis**  
  Examine how resistor and capacitor values affect transistor oscillator frequency.

- **Audio Tone Injection**  
  Feed generated audio tones from an oscillator into a transmitter and observe reception.

- **LED Flasher Timing Verification**  
  Measure frequency of a two-transistor astable multivibrator and compare with RC calculations.

- **Capacitor Value Sweep**  
  Test different capacitor values to observe effects on oscillation frequency.

- **Transistor Gain Effects**  
  Assess how transistor gain variations influence circuit stability and timing.

- **DIY Low-Power RF Beacon**  
  Construct a beacon and monitor reception distances under varying conditions.

- **Oscillator Lab**  
  Build and compare multiple oscillators for stability, frequency drift, and phase characteristics.

- **Custom Digital Mode Development**  
  Implement a microcontroller-based digital transmission system, measure reliability and reach.

- **RC Timing vs Real RF Observation**  
  Connect simple timing circuits to radio transmissions and compare predicted vs observed behavior.

- **Circuit Parameter Sensitivity Study**  
  Evaluate how changes in resistor, capacitor, and transistor values influence signal performance.
